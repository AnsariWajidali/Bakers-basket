Baker's Basket
Baker's Basket was established back in 1985 as a rustic corner store bakery selling pastries, cakes and fresh oven baked goods. Our owners & culinary experts, Ravish Arora & Rajendra Kelshikar, with a support staff of just 10 individuals, started baking out of a small yet humble kitchen located behind the Blind School in Pune. In no time, our classic Black Forest cake had become synonymous with any celebration in the city.
Over the years, with unapologetic insistence on the highest quality ingredients, artisanal craftsmanship and hard work, we have shifted to a larger factory space with 16 stores spread across Pune city and an amazing dedicated team of over 50.
